2. Action Magnitude: You may pull any lever MULTIPLE TIMES in a single 8-week block to achieve the desired magnitude of change. 

3. Levers (per economy: EUR, USD, YEN):

   - GROWTH: Each pull increases local productivity by 5%. (Effect = $1.05^n$ for $n$ pulls).

   - CONTRACTION: Each pull decreases local productivity by 5%. (Effect = $0.95^n$ for $n$ pulls).

   - DEFICIT_SPENDING: Each pull injects 5,000 currency units INTO EVERY BANK ACCOUNT in the currency zone (excluding the state's account). TOTAL INJECTION = 5,000 × (Total Accounts in Zone). Directly increases M1 and creates state debt via bank overdraft.

   - POPULATION: Each pull adds 100 household agents. (Effect = $100 \times n$). CHARACTERISTICS: These households start with ZERO wealth (empty bank accounts) but FULL labor capacity. ECONOMIC IMPACT: They immediately increase labor supply (downward pressure on wages) but require employment before they can contribute to market demand.
